Earthquake Safety Tips

1. Drop, Cover, and Hold On

If indoors, immediately drop to the ground, take cover under a sturdy table or desk, and hold on until the shaking stops.

2. Stay Away from Windows

Broken glass is a major hazard. Move away from all windows, mirrors, and hanging objects.

3. Have an Emergency Kit

Keep a kit ready with water, non-perishable food, a flashlight, a radio, and first-aid supplies.

4. If Driving, Pull Over

If driving, pull over to a clear location, stop, and stay inside your vehicle until the shaking stops.

5. Check for Injuries

After the shaking stops, check yourself and others for injuries. Administer first aid if necessary.

6. Be Prepared for Aftershocks

Aftershocks can occur minutes, hours, days, or even weeks after the main quake. Be ready to take cover again.
