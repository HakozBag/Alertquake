Earthquake Safety Tips

1. Drop, Cover, and Hold On

If indoors, immediately drop to the ground, take cover under a sturdy table or desk, and hold on to it until the shaking stops. Stay away from glass, windows, outside doors and walls.

2. Evacuation Essentials

Always keep an emergency kit ready. This includes water, non-perishable food, a first-aid kit, a flashlight, a radio, and copies of important documents. Locate your nearest evacuation center on the map.

3. After the Quake

Be prepared for aftershocks. Check yourself and others for injuries. Do not light matches or turn on switches until you are sure there are no gas leaks or damaged wires. Use your phone only for emergencies.

4. Hazard Assessment

If you are in a risk zone (near a fault line or landslide area, check the map), move to a safer location immediately. Use the 'My Route' feature for the fastest, safest evacuation path.
